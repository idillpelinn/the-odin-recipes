Joey's Meatball Sub

When Joey, Ross and Chandker take a ride-along in a cop car, it backfires. Scaring everyone and making them think they are being shot at, Joey jumps to cover Ross. Chandler becomes upset and jealous but only to find out that Joey was trying to save his meatball sub. I know it sound crazy but Joey's Meatball sub is the greatest sandwich in the world. Ingredients
  • 3/4 lb ground pork
  • 3/4 lb ground beef/li>
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oregano, roughly choopped
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, roughly choopped
  • 2 tablespoons thyme, roughly choopped
  • 2 tablespoons basil, roughly ripped
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup red wine
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 3 cups Joey'sRed Sauce or store bought marinara
  • 12 mozzarella cheese sandwich slices
  • 6 6" Italian submarine rolls
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley, roughly chopped
Steps
  1. Preheat Oven to 375Â°F
  2. In a large mixing bowl and using your hands mix together igredients. Be careful not to overmix as this will make the mixture tough.
  3. Roll out meatballs which are 2-3" in diameter. Place meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake until golden brown about 20 minutes
  4. On a stovetop and using a large pot heat marinara sauce on medium. Once meatballs are done place the meatballs in marinara sauce and allow them to soak and simmer in the sauce about 10-15 minutes
  5. While meatballs are simmering cut italians subs three quarters of the way open Place 1-2 slices of cheese inside and place the italian subs on a baking tray. Bake in the oven until cheese is soft and melted about 5 minutes.
  6. Once Italian subs are ready place 3-4 meatballs inside each sub, being sure to includes some of the sauce. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and parsley to serve.
