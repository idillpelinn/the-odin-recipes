The Joey special Two Pizzas

Joey has his own signature pizza order---it is called "The Joey Special" While we do not know specifically what type pf pizzas he orders, we know he order two. while in London, Joey calls Phoebe and when he hears that she's ordered The Joey Special he gets homesick.

Ingredient- Garlic And Clam Pizza
  • Pizza Dough
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen clam meet, thawed
  • 3 garlic cloves finely minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/3 cup freash oregano, chopped
  • Olive oil
Ingredient- Ham Spread, Sausage and Salami Pizza
  • Pizza Dough
  • 3 tablespoons Ham Spread ( ham spread repices )
  • 1/3 cup Joey's Marinara Sauce or store bought
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 10 to 13 slices salami
Steps - Garlic and Clam Pizza
  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F. If using pizza stone, plae it in the oven to get hot. roll out the pizza dough into a 13-inch circle
  2. In a small bowl pat the clams dry and mix with the garlic. Spread garlic and clam mixture out evenly over the dough
  3. Top the pizza with the Pecorino Romano and fresh oregano. Drizzle with olive oil and brush the crust with a bit of olive oil
  4. Place the pizza on a baking sheet or a pizza stone that has been heating in the oven and cook until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes
Steps - Ham Spread, Sausage and Salami Pizza
  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F. If using pizza stone, plae it in the oven to get hot. roll out the pizza dough into a 13-inch circle
  2. Spread the ham spread and marinara sacue evenly over the dough top with the mozzarella cheese, salami and crumbled saguage.
  3. Place the pizza on a baking sheet or a pizza stone that has been heating in the oven and cook until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes
